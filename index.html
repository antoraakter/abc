9 Traditional Bangladeshi Foods You Must Try

Traditional, authentic Bangladeshi cuisine is made up of a diverse range
of delicious spices, herbs, rice, fish meats and breads.

What is typical Bangladeshi food?

Similar to many other countries in Asia, Curries are one of the most popular forms of Bangladeshi food.

So without further ado, here is our list of the absolute best Bangladeshi foods to try:

  1. Hilsa/Ilish curry
  2. Biryani
  3. Chingri malai curry/Chingri Macher Malaikari
  4. Dal/Daal/Dhal
  5. Panta bhat
  6. Paratha
  7. Dhokar dalna
  8. Chachchari/Chorchori
  9. Shami kababs

Bangladeshi Street Food

Of course, the best way to really experience Bangladeshi cuisine is to actually go
there and take in the sights and smells of the food. Simply wandering the streets of the
cities you can explore the bustling markets and street food stalls to really indulge yourself!
